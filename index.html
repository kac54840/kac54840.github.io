Life at the Closet

My last day at my first Plato's Closet :/

I'm Kayla, and I work at Plato's Closet! Some people dread going to work, but I'm not one of those people! I've worked at two Plato's locations spanning the course of the past three years, and I can't imagine Plato's not being a part of my life. In fact, I hope to own my own Plato's one day!

Working at Plato's Closet is a lot crazier than you would think, and if there's one thing this job has done for me, it's toughen my skin. If you don't know what Plato's is, it's basically a high-end thrift store that pays cash on the spot for trending teen and young adult casual styles. Emphasis on trending and young adult , so as you can imagine some people don't handle rejection very well.

A Usual Convo at the Closet

Me: "Yes, I'm sorry, but today we did have to pass on everything today due to a few styles that don't sell well for us."

Agitated customer: "Are you trying say my clothes aren't in style?"

Me: *pulls out a sheer top from Chico's and a pair of 2003 low-ride American Eagle jeans*

If you would like to learn more about Plato's Closet and how to have luck selling with us (AKA make lots of money), visit https://www.platoscloset.com/ . The best way to make money at Plato's is to sell us what we're in need of.

Right now for women, we're really in need of:
  • Jeans
  • Purses (especially designer!)
  • Jewelry
  • Shoes
    • Causual Shoes (Converse, Van's, etc.)
    • Athletic Shoes (Nike, Adidas, etc.)
Right now for men, we need:
  • Jeans
  • Athletic Wear
  • Any Shoes!

There are a lot of crazy things that go on at the Closet. One of my least favorite days was one particular Sunday. It was very busy, and one of our computers was down. Then, TWO different people peed in the dressing rooms. It's important to mention that they were not children.

When is it okay for you to use the bathroom in a dressing room? When is it okay for you to use the bathroom in a dressing room and leave your mess for another customer to find?
Never! NEVER!

This is the same day an old man tried to sell us 100 bags of frozen Tyson chicken out of the back of his car.

Yeah. I don't know.

Since I spend my days sorting through people's clothes, I've found some pretty weird things in my days. Some of these include-- but are in no way limited to -- human feces, syringes, spiders, illegal drugs, gummy worms, and last but not least, bed bugs.

Yeah, Plato's is crazy, but in all seriousness, I love my job and wouldn't trade a single minute of my time at Plato's! Plato's, you have my heart! <3
